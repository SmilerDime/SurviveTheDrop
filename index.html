Survive the Drop
Championship → Premier League

Win promotion.
Then beat the drop.

Season one: draft an XI from random Championship squads of the past and fight for promotion — top two go up, 3rd–6th means the playoff lottery. Season two: your promotion route earns a budget; spend it on up to five real signings, then survive the Premier League. Or take The Long Game and see how high a multi-season project can climb.

01 Eleven spins, one signing per spin, real positions enforced (4·3·3).
02 Top 2 go up. 3rd–6th is PLAYOFFS SECURED — win two ties or the season dies at Wembley.
03 Champions £60m · runners-up £50m · playoff winners £40m. Up to 5 signings per window.
04 In The Long Game your squad ages every summer and budgets follow your finishes.

How long is
the project?

Every summer your whole squad ages (−1 rating) and the board hands you a budget based on last season's finish. Premier League money climbs the table fast; failure pays in loose change. The final score is everything you bank across the career — your best Premier League finish is the headline.
